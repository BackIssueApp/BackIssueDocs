## How it works, in one paragraph

You add a series (or import an existing library). BackIssue matches it to [ComicVine](https://comicvine.gamespot.com/), compares the issue list against the files you own, and shows you what's missing. Ask it to download the gaps — or let the scheduler do it — and it searches your enabled sources in priority order, downloads the best match, converts and tags the file, names it consistently, and files it into your library. You watch it all happen live in the queue.
